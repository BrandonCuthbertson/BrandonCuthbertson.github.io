Who Am I

Hello, I am Brandon Cuthbertson, a Junior Full-Stack Developer and videogame enthusiast who recently completed SAIT Polytechnic’s Object-Oriented Software Development course. Although my journey into coding has just begun, I am excited to peek behind the curtain of the many technologies that have provided me with wonder and delight. I am also a huge nerd, lover of fantasy and sci-fi, and hope to use my developing skills to create projects to further my passions.

Education

Southern Alberta Institute of Technology

At SAIT I completed the Fast-Tracked Object-Oriented Software Development course, a 32-week certificate program where I learned a variety of programming languages, web-based languages, and databases. As a fast-track course, it was condensed so I was very focused on gathering the information with the passion to create projects as I go forward.
